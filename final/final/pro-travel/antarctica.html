Private tour groups have been visiting Antarctica since 1969. Today there are lots of choices among operators sending ships, planes, and helicopters to Antarctica between November and March (the summer season). Many trips start in Southern Argentina, and ship-bound journeys include on-board lectures and access to experts on the ecology of the Antarctic during the cruise. Passengers can kayak around icebergs, or spend a day watching penguins, seals, and whales off the coast.

ANTARCTICA – BOAT TRIP

where to see penguinsWrap up warm and enjoy a boat trip in a zodiac – a small vessel for eight to 12 people. This allows you to explore some of the smaller passageways on the continent and get up close and personal with the icebergs and wildlife. Keep your eyes peeled for seals, penguins, whales and a whole host of birds and sea life. Larger cruise ships leave from Buenos Aires or Ushuaia, Argentina and can take in the Falkland Islands on your way to Antarctica

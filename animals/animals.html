About Animal Haven

Mission

Animal Haven is a nonprofit organization that finds homes for abandoned cats and dogs throughout the Tri-State area, and provides behavior intervention when needed to improve chances of adoption. Founded in 1967, we operate an adoption center in Manhattan and mobile adoption van. We also provide programs that enhance the bond between animals and people.
Animal Haven is a 501 (c) 3 organization.

Adoption

Cats and dogs are for adoption at Animal Haven.
Fees are:

  • $150 per cat.
  • $175 per kitten or $250 per pair.
  • $250 per dog (includes collar and leash).
  • Puppies under the age of 1 year are $350.
Fee covers spay/neuter, vaccines, microchip and license.

Applications are taken on a first-come-first basis in person.Animal Haven requests that all members of the household be present to meet the animal.

Cat adoptions are same day. Dogs may take a few days.

Animal Intake

Animal Haven does accept owner relinquishments from the public.

Cats and dogs will be given a thorough behavior and medical evaluation prior to admission.

Animal Haven does not accept animals with a history of aggression or biting or any animal over the age of eight years old because older animals do not thrive in a shelter environment.

Animal Haven reserves the right to refuse any animal. Intake hours are Saturday and Thursday from 2 to 7pm. Appointments are required.

Please call 212.274.8511 or Contact Us for more information or to make an appointment. Please note that dogs and cats must pass a behavior and medical evaluation before admitted.

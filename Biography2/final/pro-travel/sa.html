SOUTH AMERICA – CEMENTERIO DE LA RECOLETA

where to go in Buenos AiresIt may not sound like a particularly appealing holiday destination but the Cementerio de la Recoleta in Buenos Aires, Argentina is well worth a visit. You can wander for hours in the impressive cemetery, staring in amazement at the impressive statues and marble sarcophagi. Crypts in the cemetery hold the remains of some of the city’s notable former residents, from presidents and politicians to military heroes and the rich and famous. Notably, Evita’s grave is located here. Tours in English take place twice a week.

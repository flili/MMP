ASIA – STAND IN TIAN’ANMEN SQUARE

What to see in BeijingTian’anmen Square in Beijing was named after Tiananmen Gate (Gate of Heavenly Peace). It is steeped in history and an absolute must-see for anyone visiting China. The square is surrounded by a whole host of breathtaking buildings and the ultimate location for a “been there, done that” photo.
